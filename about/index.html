About Me

I'm a British-Filipino freelance web developer living in Somerset, UK, with a BSc (Hons) in Computer Science from Aberystwyth University. I first got into programming out of curiosity, starting with Python projects as a teenager and later discovering my passion for web development. Today, I create clean, responsive websites for small businesses and startups, handling everything from design to deployment.

My goal is to take the stress out of getting online. I work quickly and transparently, delivering websites that look professional, load fast, and keep your business visible to the right customers.
