The Author

Who is Lance Vincent Miguel Sebastian?

History

I spent the first half of my childhood in the streets of Manila, and the other half in the country-side of Burnham & Highbridge. This dizzying effect on my childhood resulted in my early social skills to be cryptic at best, and unfathomable at worst.

In the desert of anti-social behaviour, I found my silicon oasis: Minecraft. I was unemployed as a child, so I lived and breathed Minecraft. I quickly discovered the existence of Redstone: a mimicry of programming. I unknowingly took my first CS steps, learning how to build from YouTube.

From the Redstone Cradle, I built my foundations with Python, drawing with turtles, making text games, to my first big project: Card Company. A gamified flashcard app.
