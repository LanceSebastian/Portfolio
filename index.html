Common Questions

Scope

1

What exactly will the website include?

Every website is tailored to your needs. We clarify your goals and project requirements during an initial video call.

2

Can you help me define what I actually need?

Of course! We’ll meet to explore your goals, desired features, and design preferences so we can shape the right solution for you.

3

Do you offer UX/UI redesigns, or just development?

I offer UX/UI redesigns as a standalone service or as part of a full website build.

Pricing

4

How much will it cost?

Pricing varies based on your needs. My projects range from £300 to £1200, depending on your requirements.

5

What happens if I want changes later?

Each project includes one free revision round for adjustments or redesigns. Additional changes are available at an agreed rate.

Timeline

6

How long will it take?

Most websites are completed within 4 to 6 weeks. I’ll provide a detailed timeline once we confirm your project scope, so you know what to expect.

7

What do you need from me to get started?

I’ll need all media assets (images, videos, logos, etc.) about two weeks in. Meanwhile, I use placeholder content to maintain steady progress.

8

How do we communicate during the project?

I use email for updates and approvals, WhatsApp for quick questions, and video calls for key milestones.

Content & Control

9

Can I edit the content myself?

Absolutely! After handover, you’ll be able to update and edit your website content as needed.

10

Will you show me how to use the site?

I provide documentation covering all features with instructions. I can also walk you through any sections or offer training if needed.

11

Who owns the website and the code?

You own 100% of the website and its code. Once finished, I hand over the site and all login information for your control.

Hosting, Security & Maintenance

12

Do you provide hosting, or do I need my own?

I offer hosting as an optional service for an additional fee. Alternatively, I can build the website locally for you to host it elsewhere.

13

How do you handle security and backups?

I take security seriously. All sites are secured with SSL/TLS encryption, restricted backend access for authorized users, and trusted plugins to defend against malware and unauthorized access.

Backups are regularly and securely stored using Amazon S3, ensuring your data can be restored whenever needed.

14

Do you offer maintenance or support after launch?

Yes. I offer an optional maintenance service to keep your website secure, up to date, and running smoothly.

This includes security management, backups, software updates, performance monitoring, and minor content or design tweaks—so you can enjoy your website without worrying about technical issues.

SEO & Performance

15

Will my site rank on Google?

I design sites using SEO best practices to help you get found on Google. Ranking depends on your content and competition.

16

Do you handle SEO or analytics?

Yes. I manage both SEO and analytics for your site. Using Google Analytics, I monitor user activity and identify points where users leave the site, so I can improve the user experience and overall performance.
