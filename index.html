What I can do for you

Subtitle

  • 1. Plans & Discussions

    We meet, message, or call to discuss your goals and the features of the website (portfolio, e-commerce, or business frontend). This is so that we are both on the same page before the project starts.

  • 2. Design the Look

    I'll create the initial designs of the website, including the layout, colours, themes, and typography. Afterwards, you'll get to review the design and suggest changes before I start building the website.

  • 3. Development

    Where your website comes to life! This is where I build your approved designs into a fully functional, user-friendly website ready for the next stage!

  • 4. Add your Content

    Once the structure is ready, your images, videos, and other related materials are added into the site. If you need help preparing content, I can help guide or create materials for you.

  • 5. Testing

    We're almost there! The website is ready for testing. I go through the website's features and performance, making sure they're up to standard and working as intended through multiple devices.

  • 6. Launch Day

    Your website goes live! I handle the technical setup to ensure that the website is running smoothly and connected to relevant services, before I hand over ownership to you.

  • 7. Ongoing Help

    Though optional, I offer administrative services where I'm available for updates, maintenance, and support as your website naturally grows or your goals change.
